Departments of Excellence

Specialized care for every stage.

Each department is led by dedicated part-time staff committed to transforming lives through specialized programs and personalized care.

Ages 0–5

Early Childhood Development

The foundation of lifelong learning begins here. Research shows that 90% of brain development occurs before age 5, making these formative years critical for cognitive, emotional, and social growth.

Why It Matters Children who receive quality early education are 44% more likely to graduate from high school and 30% more likely to attend college.

Our play-based curriculum integrates sensory development, language acquisition, and spiritual foundations in a nurturing environment that honors each child's God-given potential.

Brain Development Play-Based Learning Faith Foundation
Grades 1–8

Primary Education

Since the establishment of formal education in Kenya in 1911, primary education has been the cornerstone of national development. Today's CBC framework continues this legacy while preparing participants for a rapidly changing world.

The Power of Education Every additional year of schooling increases a person's earnings by 10% and reduces poverty rates by 9%.

Our comprehensive curriculum builds strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, critical thinking, and Christian values, aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum.

CBC Aligned Critical Thinking Character Building
Forms 1–4

Secondary Education

Secondary education marks the transition from childhood to young adulthood. Historically, it has been the gateway to higher education and professional opportunities in Kenya since the 1960s.

Why Secondary Education Secondary graduates earn 50% more than primary graduates and are 3x more likely to be employed in formal sectors.

We prepare youth for KCSE excellence, university admission, and career readiness through rigorous academics, leadership training, mentorship, and Christ-centered character development.

KCSE Excellence University Prep Leadership
Skills Training

Vocational Training

Vocational education has transformed economies worldwide. In Kenya, TVET (Technical and Vocational Education and Training) graduates contribute significantly to the 83% of jobs in the informal sector.

Skills for Life Vocational training reduces youth unemployment by 60% and creates entrepreneurs who employ others, multiplying community impact.

Hands-on training in carpentry, tailoring, agriculture, IT, and entrepreneurship empowers youth with certified skills for sustainable livelihoods and self-reliance.

Certified Skills Entrepreneurship Job Ready
Faith & Character

Spiritual Formation

For over 2,000 years, faith-based education has shaped moral character and ethical leadership. Studies show that spiritual development correlates with 40% higher life satisfaction and resilience.

Why Spiritual Growth Children with strong moral foundations are 70% less likely to engage in risky behaviors and 85% more likely to become community leaders.

Through age-appropriate discipleship, worship, biblical teaching, and service opportunities, we help participants know Christ, develop character, and make Him known in their communities.

Discipleship Moral Character Service
Wellbeing

Health & Nutrition

The connection between health and learning is undeniable. Research shows that malnourished children score 15% lower on tests and are 4x more likely to repeat grades.

Healthy Children Learn Better School feeding programs increase enrollment by 25% and improve academic performance by 20%.

We ensure physical wellbeing through regular health screenings, nutritious meals, hygiene education, disease prevention, and access to medical care — because a healthy body supports a healthy mind.

Nutrition Health Screening Disease Prevention
Physical Fitness

Sports & Recreation

Since the ancient Greek Olympics in 776 BC, sports have been recognized as essential for developing discipline, teamwork, and leadership. Modern research confirms that physically active participants perform 20% better academically.

More Than Games Participant athletes develop 25% better time management skills and are 15% more likely to graduate from college.

Through organized sports, games, and outdoor activities, we promote physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and leadership — building character that lasts a lifetime.

Teamwork Discipline Leadership
Creative Expression

Arts & Culture

From prehistoric cave paintings to modern digital art, creative expression has been fundamental to human civilization. Studies show that arts education improves critical thinking by 30% and cultural awareness by 45%.

Creativity Matters Participants engaged in arts are 4x more likely to be recognized for academic achievement and 3x more likely to show leadership initiative.

Through music, dance, visual arts, drama, and cultural preservation, we celebrate creativity and heritage while developing artistic gifts and authentic self-expression.

Music & Dance Visual Arts Cultural Heritage
Emotional Care

Counseling & Support

Mental health awareness has grown significantly since the 1950s. Today, we know that participants who receive emotional support are 60% more resilient and 50% more successful in relationships and careers.

Healing Hearts Trauma-informed care reduces behavioral issues by 70% and improves academic performance by 35% in affected participants.

We provide emotional support, trauma healing, life skills training, and mental health resources in a safe, confidential environment that honors each participant's dignity and journey toward wholeness.

Trauma Healing Life Skills Safe Space
Community Impact

Community Outreach

Community development has transformed societies worldwide. Research shows that every $1 invested in community education generates $4 in economic returns and reduces poverty by 25%.

Ripple Effect Educated community members are 80% more likely to volunteer, 65% more likely to vote, and 90% more likely to support other children's education.

Through parent engagement, community projects, advocacy, and partnership development, we extend our impact beyond our centre, creating sustainable change that multiplies our mission throughout Mfangano Island and beyond.

Family Engagement Community Projects Sustainable Impact
