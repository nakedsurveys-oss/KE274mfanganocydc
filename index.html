Who We Are

A foundation built on faith, love, and unwavering commitment.

For nearly a decade, we have walked alongside the most vulnerable children on Mfangano Island — nurturing every dimension of their development so they can become self-reliant, Christ-centered adults who transform their communities.

Our Mission

To holistically release needy children and youth from poverty in Jesus' name. We nurture every aspect of a participant's growth — spiritually, physically, socially, emotionally, and economically — from pregnancy through young adulthood.

Our Vision

A future where every participant grows into a fulfilled Christian adult who knows Christ, makes Him known, and is self-reliant — empowered to reach their full potential and become self-sustained members of their communities.

Our Story

Rooted in faith. Transforming lives since 2017.

What began as a partnership between Sena PEFA Church and Compassion International Kenya has grown into a movement of holistic transformation across Mfangano Island and the surrounding communities of Suba West.

Sena Centre, PEFA Cathedral Sena · Suba West
Location & Partnership

A Strategic Alliance

Based at Sena Centre, PEFA Cathedral Sena in Suba West Sub-County, Homa Bay County — a strategic partnership between Sena PEFA Church and Compassion International Kenya serving the island communities of Lake Victoria.

July — August 2017

Partnership Signed, Program Launched

The partnership agreement was signed on July 1, 2017, and the program officially launched on August 15, 2017. The first cohort of participants was enrolled in early 2018, marking the beginning of a new chapter for hundreds of families.

Our Purpose

Holistic Child Development

Addressing the spiritual, physical, socio-economic, and cognitive needs of children and youth through integrated programs in agriculture, entrepreneurship, WASH (Water, Sanitation & Hygiene), and environmental conservation.
