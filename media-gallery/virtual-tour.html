How to Reach Sena Centre

Everything you need to know for your visit to Mfangano Island and KE0274 PEFA Mfangano CYDC.

Ferry & Boat Routes

  • Mbita – Mfangano Ferry Main ferry service from Mbita Point to Mfangano Island. Runs multiple times daily. Journey takes approximately 15–20 minutes.
  • Private Boat Transport Private boats available at Mbita landing. Negotiate fare in advance. Good for groups or flexible schedules.
  • Operating Hours Ferry services typically run from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Confirm schedules locally as they may vary by season.

Route Guidance

  • From Nairobi Drive to Kisumu (~6 hrs), then to Homa Bay (~2 hrs), then to Mbita Point (~1 hr). Take ferry to Mfangano Island.
  • From Kisumu Drive west to Homa Bay (~2 hrs), continue to Mbita Point (~1 hr). Ferry crossing to Mfangano Island.
  • From Homa Bay Drive to Mbita Point (~1 hr). Take the ferry or private boat to Mfangano Island. Sena Centre is accessible from the landing.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit Dry seasons (January–March, June–October) offer the best weather. Saturday programs run 9 AM – 2 PM during school terms.
  • What to Bring Comfortable walking shoes, sun protection, light clothing, and a sense of adventure. The island is warm year-round.
  • Communication Mobile network coverage is available (Safaricom, Airtel). Internet access is limited — plan accordingly.
