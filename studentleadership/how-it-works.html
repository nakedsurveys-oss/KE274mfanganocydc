About This System

This digital voting platform ensures fair, secure, and transparent student elections. Every vote is encrypted, counted automatically, and displayed in real-time to authorized administrators.

🔐 One Vote Per Student: Each voter uses a unique KE number (001–550). Once submitted, the system locks your ID to prevent duplicate voting.

Step-by-Step Voting Process

1
Start at Home Page

Visit the election portal and click "Vote Now" to begin your voting journey.

2
Verify Your Identity

Enter your 3-digit KE number (001–550) to authenticate your voter eligibility.

3
Review Candidates

For each of the 10 positions, review candidate profiles and select your preferred choice.

4
Confirm Selections

Review all your choices before final submission. You can go back to change any selection.

5
Submit Your Vote

Click "Submit Vote" to securely record your ballot. Your KE number is then marked as voted.

6
View Live Results

Admins can view real-time, aggregated results on the secure dashboard (voters cannot see results until voting closes).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a KE number and how do I get one?
Your KE number is a unique 3-digit identifier (001–550) assigned to each eligible voter by the election committee. Check your student portal or contact your class representative if you don't know yours.
Can I change my vote after submitting?
No. For security and fairness, votes cannot be changed once submitted. Please review your selections carefully before clicking "Submit Vote".
Is my vote anonymous?
Yes. While your KE number is used to verify eligibility and prevent duplicate voting, your individual choices are encrypted and never linked to your identity in the results display.
When will results be published?
Results are visible to administrators in real-time during voting. Official results will be published to all students after the voting period closes and results are verified by the election committee.
What if I have technical issues?
Try refreshing the page or using a different browser. If problems persist, contact the IT support team at support@elections2026.school with your KE number and a description of the issue.
