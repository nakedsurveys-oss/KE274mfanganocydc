Our Philosophy

Leadership as service, not status.

At KE0274 PEFA Mfangano CYDC, we believe leadership is not a title — it is a posture of the heart. Our student council is formed not to rule, but to serve; not to be seen, but to lift others up. Every leader is first a learner, first a servant, first a child of God.

Faith-Led

Every decision grounded in Christ-like character and biblical wisdom.

Service-Driven

Authority expressed through meeting the needs of others.

Integrity-First

Consistency between word and deed, in public and in private.

Growth-Oriented

Leaders are continually learning, mentored, and empowered.

Every child has leadership potential.

Through mentorship, responsibility, and service opportunities, we nurture confident, compassionate leaders who will transform their communities for Christ. The council you see today is the fruit of a culture where every voice matters and every gift is honored.
