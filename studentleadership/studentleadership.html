Meet Your Leaders

Elected by students, guided by mentors, committed to service

Stanley Okinyi
Student President

Stanley leads the council with a vision for unity and excellence. He presides over meetings, represents the student body in administrative discussions, and ensures that every voice is heard.

Mercy Amelie
Deputy President

Mercy supports the President and oversees internal council harmony. She focuses on conflict resolution and ensuring that council decisions are implemented effectively across all grades.

Thomas Odhiambo
Prime Minister

Thomas acts as the chief coordinator of council activities. He serves as the primary link between academic staff and student leaders, ensuring smooth communication and collaboration.

Emanuel Isaac
Welfare & Child Protection

Emanuel is dedicated to the safety and well-being of every child. He handles peer conflicts, reports safeguarding concerns, and ensures a safe, inclusive environment for all students.

Rooney Odero
Sports & Games Captain

Rooney manages sports equipment and organizes leagues. He promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and fair play during inter-class and inter-school competitions.

Cyprian Awiti
Religious Leader

Cyprian leads the student body in spiritual matters. He organizes chapel programs, leads the choir, and encourages moral uprightness and integrity among students.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our leadership and service

Service

We lead by serving others, putting the needs of our community before our own interests.

Integrity

We act with honesty and transparency, building trust through consistent ethical behavior.

Inclusivity

We welcome every voice and ensure that every student feels valued and represented.

Faith

We ground our leadership in Christian values, seeking wisdom and guidance in all we do.
