Rotating Weekly

Saturday special programs.

Unique programs running from 2:15 – 3:00 PM each Saturday for holistic development.

1st Saturday

Mentorship & Leadership Training

Empowering participants aged 12+ with leadership skills, mentorship, and character development for future impact.

2nd Saturday

Sanitation & Reproductive Health

ARISE program promoting hygiene, health awareness, and reproductive education in a safe, supportive environment.

3rd Saturday

Discipleship Sessions

Deepening faith through biblical teaching, worship, and spiritual formation guided by trained mentors.

4th Saturday

Birthday Celebrations

Celebrating our participants' special days with joy, recognition, and community fellowship.
